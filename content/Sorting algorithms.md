## Speeds
> [!NOTE]- Sorting algorithm speeds:
> bubble sort $\dfrac{(n^2 - n)}{2}$
> insertion sort: $\dfrac{n^2}{2}$
> selection sort: $\dfrac{(n^2 - -n)}{2}$
> merge sort: $nlogn$
